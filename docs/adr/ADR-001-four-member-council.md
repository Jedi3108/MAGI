# ADR-001: Four Permanent Council Members

## Status

Accepted

## Context

MAGI requires stable reasoning identities that users can understand, test, and trust over time.

## Decision

MAGI will use four permanent council members:

- MELCHIOR
- BALTHASAR
- CASPER
- ARTABAN

These identities remain stable even if the underlying AI models change.

## Consequences

- The council has balanced perspectives.
- Deadlocks are possible.
- A deliberation and tie-resolution protocol is required.
